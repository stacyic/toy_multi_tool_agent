# Company Policies

## Return Policy

Customers may return products within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in original packaging with receipt.

### Category-Specific Rules
- **Electronics**: 15-day return window due to rapid depreciation. A 15% restocking fee applies.
- **Apparel**: 30-day return window. Items must be unworn with tags attached.
- **Outdoor Equipment**: 30-day return window. Items must be unused.
- **Home Goods**: 30-day return window for unused items.
- **Accessories**: 30-day return window.

### Exceptions
- Clearance items are final sale and cannot be returned
- Custom orders cannot be returned
- Software licenses are non-refundable once activated
- Items marked "Final Sale" cannot be returned

## Shipping Policy

### Domestic Shipping
- **Standard Shipping**: 5-7 business days
- **Express Shipping**: 2-3 business days (additional fee applies)
- **Free Shipping**: Available on orders over $50

### International Shipping
We ship to Canada and Mexico. International orders may incur customs fees. Delivery times vary by destination (typically 10-14 business days).

## Customer Tiers and Benefits

### VIP Customer Definition
A VIP customer is defined as anyone who has spent over $1,000 in the last 12 months. VIP benefits include:
- 10% discount on all purchases
- Free express shipping
- Early access to sales
- Dedicated customer support line

### Regular Customer
Customers who have not reached VIP status. Benefits include:
- Standard shipping rates
- Access to regular promotions
- Email support

## Privacy Policy

We collect minimal personal information necessary for order processing. Customer data is never sold to third parties. Customers may request data deletion at any time by contacting support.

### Data We Collect
- Name and contact information
- Shipping and billing addresses
- Order history
- Payment information (securely stored)

## Order Status Definitions

- **Placed**: Order received, payment pending or confirmed
- **Shipped**: Order has left our warehouse
- **Delivered**: Order confirmed delivered to customer
- **Cancelled**: Order was cancelled before shipping
- **Returned**: Order was returned by customer
